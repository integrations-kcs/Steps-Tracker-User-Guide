## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **iOS**: iOS 14.0 or later
- **Android**: Android 14.0 or later with Health Connect support

### Installation

1. Download the Steps Tracker app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
2. Install the app on your device
3. Open the app to begin registration

---

## Registration

When you first launch the app, you'll see the Welcome screen.

### Registration Steps

1. **Device Information**: The app automatically detects your device information including:
   - Device ID
   - Device OS version
   - Device Model

2. **Employee ID** (Required)
   - Enter your employee ID in the format: `K` followed by 6 digits
   - Example: `K123456`
   - This ID is provided by your employer

3. **Profile Name** (Required)
   - Enter your full name as you'd like it to appear in the app
   - This name will be visible on leaderboards

4. **Complete Registration**
   - Tap the **Register** button to create your account
   - You'll be prompted to grant health data permissions

---

## Permissions Setup

To track your steps accurately, the app needs permission to access your device's health data.

### For iOS Users

1. After registration, you'll be prompted to allow health data access
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy & Security** > **Health**
3. Select **Steps Tracker**
4. Enable **Steps** under "Allow Steps Tracker to Read"
5. The app will now sync with Apple HealthKit to retrieve your step data

### For Android Users

1. After registration, you'll be prompted to allow health data access
2. The app will redirect you to **Health Connect**
3. Grant permission for Steps Tracker to read **Steps** data
4. Return to the app to begin tracking

**Note**: The app requires these permissions to function. Without access to health data, step tracking will not work.

## Dashboard

The Dashboard provides an overview of the entire team's progress and competitive standings.

### Features

#### Total Steps
- Displays the cumulative steps walked by Kuok Group
- Updates in real-time as employees log steps

#### Fundraising Progress
- **Fund Raised**: Shows the current amount raised based on total steps
- **Target**: Displays the fundraising goal ($300,000.00)
- **Conversion Rate**: $0.01 per 10 steps walked
- Progress bar indicates percentage toward goal

#### Your Current Rank
- Shows your ranking among all participating employees
- Displays total number of participants
- Updates as you and others accumulate steps

#### Top 20 Leaderboard
- View the top 20 step leaders in your organization
- Each entry shows:
  - Rank position (with color-coded badges for top 3)
  - Employee name
  - Total steps accumulated
- Your position is highlighted with "(You)" indicator
- Leaderboard updates regularly to reflect current standings

### Navigation
- Access Dashboard by tapping the **Dashboard** icon in the bottom navigation bar

## Individual Progress

The Individual Progress screen shows your personal step tracking and achievements.

### Today's Steps

#### Main Display
- **Current Steps**: Large display of today's step count
- **Daily Goal**: Your target steps (default: 10,000)
- **Progress Bar**: Visual representation of goal completion
- **Steps to Goal**: Shows remaining steps needed
- **Percentage**: Displays goal completion percentage

### Statistics

#### My Steps Contribution
- Shows your cumulative contribution to the team total
- All-time step count since registration

#### Longest Goal Streak
- Tracks consecutive days you've met your daily goal
- Resets to 0 if you miss a day
- Encourages consistency in activity

### Last 7 Days

#### Weekly Overview
- Bar chart showing daily step counts for the past week
- Each bar represents one day with the step count displayed
- Star icons above each day (for achievements/goal completion)
- Dates labeled below each bar
- Helps identify patterns and trends in your activity

### Features
- **Refresh**: Tap the refresh icon (top right) to sync latest data
- **Auto-Update**: Data refreshes automatically when you open the tab

## Troubleshooting

### Steps Not Syncing

**iOS**:
1. Open **Settings** > **Privacy & Security** > **Health**
2. Ensure Steps Tracker has permission to read Steps
3. Check that Apple Health is recording steps (open Health app)
4. Restart the Steps Tracker app

**Android**:
1. Open **Health Connect** app
2. Check that Steps Tracker has permission to read Steps data
3. Ensure Health Connect is properly recording step data
4. Restart the Steps Tracker app

### Data Not Updating

1. Pull down on the screen to manually refresh
2. Tap the refresh icon in the top right corner
3. Check your internet connection
4. Ensure the app has background refresh enabled in device settings
5. Force close and reopen the app

### Can't See My Rank

1. Ensure you've completed registration
2. Verify that you have accumulated steps
3. Check that you have an active internet connection
4. Rankings update periodically, so wait a few minutes and refresh


### Registration Issues

1. Ensure your Employee ID follows the correct format (K followed by 6 digits)
2. Check that you've entered all required fields
3. Verify you have a stable internet connection
