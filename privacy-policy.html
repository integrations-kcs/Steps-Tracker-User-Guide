Privacy Policy

KGSg Step Up For Good Mobile Application

Last Updated: 7 January 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kuok Group Singapore ("Company") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in connection with the KGSg Step Up For Good mobile application ("App").

The Company is committed to respecting your privacy and handling personal data responsibly.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies only to the App and does not cover external websites, platforms, or services that may be linked to or integrated with the App.

3. Personal Data Collected

The App may collect the following categories of data:

  • Step count and activity metrics
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device and operating system information
  • Signup identifiers (e.g. Signup ID), where required for access control

The App does not collect precise location data, unless explicitly stated within the App.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

Personal data is collected and processed solely for:

  • Operating and maintaining the App
  • Supporting internal wellness or engagement initiatives
  • Producing aggregated or anonymised internal reports
  • Improving App functionality, performance, and security

5. Legal Basis and Internal Use

Data is processed for legitimate internal business and participant engagement purposes.

The App is not intended for public users or commercial profiling.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on Company-managed systems or authorised service providers.
  • Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to protect data.
  • Access to identifiable data is limited to authorised personnel only.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App does not use cookies in the traditional web sense.

Limited technical identifiers may be used solely to ensure functionality, performance, and security, and do not identify users personally.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

The Company does not sell or trade personal data.

Personal data may be disclosed only:

  • Where required by law or regulatory authorities
  • To internal teams administering the App or wellness programmes
  • To service providers strictly for operational purposes and under confidentiality obligations

9. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Policy or to comply with legal or internal requirements.

Upon App deactivation, data may be deleted or anonymised in accordance with Company policies.

10. Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for ensuring that any device used to access the App is properly secured and for keeping your login credentials confidential.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Company may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by publishing an updated version. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

12. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy shall be governed by the laws of Singapore.

13. Language

In the event of any inconsistency or ambiguity between language versions, the English version shall prevail.
